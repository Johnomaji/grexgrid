02 — Visual Metaphor

The governing idea

Every design decision — logo, grid, icon, motion, pattern — is generated from one master metaphor. Five candidates were interrogated. One was chosen by reason, not preference.

The Circuit

Precision, flow, interconnection. Too generic — could be any fintech or tech company. Reads as cold, mechanical. Does not carry the human warmth GrēxGrid requires in its last-mile communities.

The Cell

Organic, self-replicating, alive. Strong for the biological dimension but lacks the network and community trading dimension. Too micro — GrēxGrid operates at community scale.

Chosen
The Murmuration

A flock moving as one — distributed intelligence, no central controller, emergent behaviour. "Grex" is Latin for flock. Each community is a node. The whole system moves as one. Intelligence is distributed. The pattern is ancient (African constellations, navigation) and modern (network topology). This generates every visual element from the same DNA.

The Market

Exchange, human density, value creation. Strong for the trading dimension (GrēxShare) but chaotic as a master metaphor. Markets have noise — GrēxGrid has intelligence.

The Root System

Underground, invisible infrastructure, feeding everything above. Beautiful as a secondary metaphor. Communicates permanence but can read as hidden — GrēxGrid needs to be visible and legible to communities.

The murmuration generates: the logo form (nodes in formation), the grid system (organic spacing, not rigid), the icon set (built from the same stroke and node vocabulary), the pattern language (flowing node constellations), and the motion principles (responsive, fluid, never mechanical).

Status badges
Sharp (0) for data and structural elements — grids, dashboards, billing tables. Minimal (2px) for content cards. Soft (8px) for interactive elements — buttons, inputs. Pill for status indicators only.
Motion Tokens
Instant
0ms · State changes
Swift
150ms ease-out · UI feedback
Flow
300ms ease-in-out · Page transitions
Settle
500ms spring · Data loading, charts
Swift for any interaction response — button click, tap, input focus. Flow for page and panel transitions. Settle for numbers counting up, charts rendering — energy data should feel like it arrives, not just appears.
Elevation
Level 0 — No elevation. Content on surface. Data tables, grids.
Level 1 — Cards, panels. Background shifts from parchment to white.
Level 2 — Active/focused cards. Border weight increases.
Level 3 — Modals, overlays. Night background. Highest priority.
GrēxGrid uses no drop shadows. Elevation is communicated through background value (darker = lower, lighter = higher) and border weight. This ensures the system works at 3am on a dim screen in Kano.

The solution

Five products. One platform. Complete energy sovereignty.

GrēxGrid is not a solar panel company. It is a complete energy operating system — built for communities that cannot wait.

GrēxBox
Plug-and-play solar + battery storage hardware. Installed in your community, owned by your community.
GrēxShare
Peer-to-peer energy trading. Surplus power sold to neighbours. Your community earns from its grid.
GrēxPay
Mobile money + USSD pay-as-you-go. Works on any phone. Accessible to every member of your community.
GrēxSense
AI-driven monitoring and optimisation. Predicts faults, maximises trading, tracks impact in real time.
GrēxConnect
API layer for governments, NGOs, and energy deployers. Integrate GrēxGrid data into any system.
How it works

From installation to income, in four steps.

GrēxGrid is designed to be understood, owned, and operated by communities — not dependent on technical staff or imported expertise.

01
Install GrēxBox
Our team installs the solar-plus-storage hardware at your community site. Setup completes in under 4 hours. No grid connection required.
02
Connect your households
Each household registers via the app or USSD. No smartphone required. Credit is loaded via mobile money — M-Pesa, Opay, PalmPay.
03
Generate and use
Your community begins generating. The GrēxSense AI optimises distribution in real time. You pay only for what you use.
04
Trade and earn
Surplus energy is traded to neighbouring nodes. Your community earns credit — reducing bills, building a shared energy fund.
